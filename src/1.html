I really do want to try out Tzolk'in: The Mayan Calendar too.



It was fun! But we didn't make it to play terra mystica and tzolkin because karnag lasted a bit long.

Karnag is a kind of worker placement game where you move with your druids around a forest und try to keep monsters away and try to build menhirs. It's really hard to describe and we weren't that happy with the game since the rules are not that well written and the theme seems a bit stupid (druids that build stones with their energy beams) WTF.

We were also only 3 players and I think Karnag ist better with 4 or 5 palyers. I will play it again in a bigger round the next weekend and I think I'm going to write a review after this. So maybe than I can get the idea of the game better catched with words ;-)


You should really try Tzolkin since it is a really great game!


I played Karnag in Essen. During/After the rules explanation I had the feeling that this game wouldn't be for me, we stopped playing about halfway in, no one (4 people, two of which I didn't know) particularly liked the game.


Good to hear that you both do not like it. I thought about preordering it before Essen and now I am happy that I did not.
